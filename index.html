How It Works

Four simple steps from submitting a complaint to getting it fully resolved.

01

Student Registers Complaint

Student fills in the complaint form with name, roll number, department, category and a clear description.

02

Admin Reviews Complaint

Admin verifies the complaint, checks its validity and updates its status from New to Under Review.

03

Assigned to Department

Complaint is routed to the correct department — IT, Facilities, Academics, or Administration for action.

04

Resolved & Status Updated

Once resolved, the status is updated in real time. Student can track and see the full resolution notes.

About The System

Built to Improve Transparency in College

The Smart College Complaint Management System is a digital platform designed to modernize the way students report issues to college authorities. Traditional complaint boxes and informal requests often lead to unresolved problems and a lack of accountability.

Our system creates a structured, transparent and trackable process where every complaint has a unique ID, an assigned owner, and a clear resolution timeline — ensuring nothing is ignored.

  • 100% digital — no paper-based complaints
  • Unique complaint ID for easy tracking
  • Auto-routing to correct department
  • Admin dashboard with full control
  • Image upload support for evidence
  • Secure data storage with privacy protection
